Government austerity, delivery costs, aging populations, rampant chronic disease and patient demands are combining to challenge health and social care systems around the world. Observers describe the situation as a ‘perfect storm’ of crisis, but also of opportunity.

RecapHealth invests and actively supports world class opportunities and is fortunate to work with partners that include healthcare entrepreneurs, executives, providers, private payers, non profits and governments alike.

Portfolio

Medeo is a communications platform that allows patients, via secure video on computer, iphone or iPad, to see a licensed physician using their existing medical services coverage.

Tyze Personal Networks is a globally recognized social enterprise "B" Corp which provides technology solutions to help families, friends and care professionals to work together to improve health outcomes for vulnerable populations including the elderly, disabled and chronically ill. Tyze is a mission based organization that works with leading foundations and non profits as well as more traditional financial and commercial partners. RecapHealthVentures' Rich Osborn acted as Interim President and Chief Operating Officer while leading the company’s financing efforts, UK expansion and strategic partnership relationships. Tyze was sold to St Elizabeth Home Health Care, One of Canada's largest home care providers, and itself a social enterprise, in December 2013.

Thoughtwire’s software delivers immediate business value by rapidly interconnecting software, people and information they need in a more adaptable, scaleable and focused way. The Thoughtwire Unified Experience Platform was purpose built to bridge the user experience, information and workflow gaps that plague the healthcare system. Thoughtwire has received many industry awards and recognition and was recently named one of Canada's Top 25 ICT Companies.

CML Global Healthcare is the leading diagnostic imaging provider in Canada with more than 100 sites across Ontario and British Columbia. In September 2013 CML was acquired by LifeLabs Ontario in an all cash transaction valued at $1.2 Billion.

QHR (TSX:QHR), develops and sells powerful, integrated healthcare management software designed specifically for Canadian Healthcare and Social Services organizations. Its core offerings are focused on Electronic Medical Records, Enterprise Management Systems and Revenue Cycle Management. Recap sold its position in QHR in 2014.

Health Innovations Group is a RecapHealth incubated venture designed to foster greater collaboration between health care providers and technology based solutions providers. It will work to solve specific problem sets for clinicians and public payers in an accelerated and cost effective manner.

YYoga is a rapidly growing chain of yoga and wellness centers. Headquartered in Vancouver, BC YYoga was founded by Terry McBride a well known Canadian music entrepreneur. YYoga centers offer a ‘spa-like’ experience with yoga, fitness and wellness services. RecapHealthVentures acted as investor and advisor to YYoga in its recent $13M financing, which included new equity from private equity funds and high net worth individuals, as well as debt conversion and the acquisition of three additional centers. YYoga is now Canada's largest yoga chain with operations in BC and Ontario.

Co-Patient has developed a proprietary series of algorithms to intelligently and proactively identify errors (i.e. over-charging) in medical bills. An estimated 60% of medical bills contain a major error, which leads directly to major system inefficiencies as well as end consumer pain-and in many cases outright bankruptcies. RecapHealth is working to help Co-Patient in channel and sales development as well as introductions with foundation and endowment partners for focus on vulnerable population segments.

Led by noted Canadian entrepreneur Angus Reid, Vision Critical is the global leader in the provision of software and services that are revolutionizing consumer engagement. Focused on industries such as healthcare and citizen consultation Vision Critical is one of Canada's leading private companies and the 5 largest technology company in BC.
