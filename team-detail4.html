Jose Toussaint

Associate

Jose is an Associate with RecapHealth Ventures. He focuses on screening new investment opportunities as well as leading the company's efforts in collecting a pan-Canadian data set of all companies involved in health and wellness.

Jose has over 7 years of experience at Novartis Pharmaceuticals, one of the top 3 pharma companies worldwide, and has worked in both market research and marketing roles leading the launch of new blockbuster products as well as efforts focused on sales and marketing efforts for more established brands. While at Novartis Jose was awarded the Global Innovation award for patient centric initiatives that could potentially revolutionize healthcare management for patients with Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD).

While completing his MBA at UBC, Jose worked with the Aravind Eye Care hospital (Madurai, India) and Fraser Health Authority in Vancouver to perform a comparative analysis on cataract surgery, leading to international collaboration for improving healthcare delivery.
