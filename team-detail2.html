Dr. Samuel J. Gutman

MD CCFP (Emergency), Partner

Dr. Gutman is a Venture Partner with RecapHealth Ventures. A practicing emergency room physician and accomplished entrepreneur, Dr. Gutman is the rare combination of an experienced medical clinician who also understands business and has a proven track record of success.

Over his career, Dr. Gutman has been entrusted to care for a wide range of complex patients and situations for world class athletes, entertainers, and corporate leaders as well as those in need. He is the President and Founder of Rockdoc, a provider of specialized medical services and health care consulting for events and businesses across Canada. Rockdoc's customer list includes the 2010 Olympic Winter Games, Ironman Canada, Tough Mudder and Live Nation Entertainment. Dr. Gutman was previously the co-founder of a successful chain of personalized medical and wellness clinics based in Vancouver BC with locations in Alberta and Ontario AND CAREVOYANCE? .

Following graduation with honours from the University of Western Ontario School of Medicine in 1991, Dr. Gutman has been a practicing Emergency Physician working in major trauma receiving and referral hospitals throughout Vancouver, BC.
